About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Yeshiva University focused on building secure, privacy-preserving, and trustworthy AI/ML for real-world, high-dimensional data. My work integrates federated learning, fuzzy clustering, and visualization-aided validation with applications in digital health, IoT/CPS, and data systems for AI.

  • Ph.D., Computer & Information Science — UMass Dartmouth
  • 2021 IEEE ComSoc MCTC Best Paper Award
  • NSF/NIH project experience (privacy-aware analytics; cooperative perception/V2X)
